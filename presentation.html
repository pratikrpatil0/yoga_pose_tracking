Key Algorithms & Logic

  • Pose Detection: MoveNet SINGLEPOSE_THUNDER model for accurate keypoint tracking
  • Accuracy Calculation: Euclidean distance between detected and target keypoints
  • Confidence Filtering: Minimum 20% confidence threshold for reliable detection
  • Intelligent Feedback: Analyzes specific body parts and provides targeted corrections
  • Normalization: Adjusts for different body sizes and camera distances
